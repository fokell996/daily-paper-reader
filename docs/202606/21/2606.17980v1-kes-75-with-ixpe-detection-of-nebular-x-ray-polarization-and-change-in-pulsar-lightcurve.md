## 摘要
我们首次对SNR Kes 75内的PSR/PWN复合体进行了X射线偏振测量。两次约500 ks的IXPE观测分别于2024年10月/11月和2025年4月进行。第二次观测在2-8 keV能段测得显著的相位平均偏振度PD = 9.9% ± 2.5%，偏振角PA=36.8° ± 7.3°，暗示存在与PWN对称轴对齐的环形磁场。然而，第一次观测仅得到偏振上限。在该次观测期间，在Δφ≈0.5处可见额外的脉冲成分，检测显著性约3.7σ。无分箱相位分辨分析显示，在约99.5%置信水平下存在高PD的旋转矢量模型PA扫描，其角度固定为根据PWN形态推断的值；这可以解释相位平均偏振的缺失。需要额外的观测来确定异常脉冲的性质。

## Abstract
We present the first X-ray polarization measurements of the PSR/PWN complex within SNR Kes 75. Two $\rm {\sim}\,500\,ks$ IXPE observations were conducted in October/November 2024 and April 2025. The second observation yields a significant phase-average 2-8 keV polarization degree $\rm PD = 9.9\% \pm 2.5\%$ at $\rm PA=36.8^\circ \pm 7.3^\circ$, implying a toroidal field aligned with the PWN symmetry axis. The first epoch, however, has only a polarization upper limit. During this epoch, an additional pulsed component is visible at $Δφ\approx 0.5$, detected at ${\sim}\,3.7σ$. An unbinned phase-resolved analysis reveals a high-PD rotating vector model PA sweep at the ${\sim}\,99.5\%$ confidence level, with angles fixed at those inferred from the PWN morphology; this can explain the loss of phase-average polarization. Additional observations are needed to pin down the nature of the anomalous pulse.
