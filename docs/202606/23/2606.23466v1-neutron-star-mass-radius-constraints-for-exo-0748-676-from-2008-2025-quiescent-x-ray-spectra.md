## 摘要
我们通过对2008年至2025年期间获得的20次静态X射线观测（包括14次钱德拉和6次XMM-Newton曝光）的联合分析，提出了对中子星低质量X射线双星EXO 0748-676中中子星质量和半径的新约束。这些数据采样了由2024-2025年爆发分隔的两个静态时期。我们使用氢大气模型对0.5-10 keV光谱进行建模，假设源距离为7.1 kpc。在全局马尔可夫链蒙特卡洛分析中，所有观测的氢柱密度、中子星质量和半径被联合约束，我们得到中子星质量为$1.77^{+0.17}_{-0.22}\,M_\odot$，半径为$12.62^{+0.56}_{-0.74}$ km（1σ置信区间）。我们进一步对第一和第二个静态时期进行独立拟合，发现组合数据集显著减小了后验分布中的低质量尾部，从而收紧了对中子星质量的下限。结合$7.1\pm1.2$ kpc的距离不确定性，我们保守地将中子星质量和半径约束为$M\simeq 1.41-2.11~M_{\odot}$和$R\simeq 10.15-15.13$ km，倾向于相对较硬的致密物质状态方程。我们还追踪了两个静态时期的热演化，发现了2024-2025年爆发后重新开始的壳层冷却证据，为比较两次不同吸积事件后的热弛豫行为提供了独特机会。

## Abstract
We present new constraints on the mass and radius of the neutron star in the neutron star low-mass X-ray binary EXO 0748$-$676 obtained from a joint analysis of 20 quiescent X-ray observations obtained between 2008 and 2025, including 14 Chandra and 6 XMM-Newton exposures. These data sample two quiescent episodes separated by the 2024$-$2025 outburst. We model the 0.5$-$10 keV spectra with a hydrogen-atmosphere model, assuming a source distance of 7.1 kpc. In a global Markov Chain Monte Carlo analysis in which the hydrogen column density, neutron star mass, and radius are tied across all observations, we obtain a neutron-star mass of $1.77^{+0.17}_{-0.22}\,M_\odot$ and a radius of $12.62^{+0.56}_{-0.74}$ km ($1σ$ credible intervals). We further perform independent fits to the first and second quiescent epochs and find that the combined data set significantly reduces the low-mass tail in the posterior distribution, leading to tighter lower bounds on the neutron-star mass. Incorporating the distance uncertainty of $7.1\pm1.2$ kpc, we conservatively constrain the neutron-star mass and radius to $M\simeq 1.41-2.11~M_{\odot}$ and $R\simeq 10.15-15.13$ km, favoring relatively stiff dense-matter equations of state. We also trace the thermal evolution across two quiescent epochs and find evidence for renewed crust cooling following the 2024$-$2025 outburst, providing a unique opportunity to compare the thermal relaxation behavior after two distinct accretion episodes.
