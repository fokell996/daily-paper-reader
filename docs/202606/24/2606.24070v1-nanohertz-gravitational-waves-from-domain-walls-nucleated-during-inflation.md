## 摘要
我们研究了由暴胀期间通过量子隧穿成核的畴壁（DW）产生的标量诱导引力波（SIGW），成核时间较长。与短周期成核框架相反，短周期成核框架中畴壁瞬时形成，产生的曲率功率谱太弱，无法解释脉冲星计时阵列（PTA）合作组报告的纳赫兹随机引力波背景（SGWB），我们证明有限成核持续时间导致畴壁半径分布具有特征γ≡R^4的平均/(R^2的平均)^2 > 1，增强了产生的曲率扰动。我们构建了一个双场暴胀模型，包含暴胀子φ和 spectators场χ，通过势V(φ,χ)耦合，其中畴壁张力σ(t)随着暴胀子滚过临界值而平滑演化。这个转变的特征宽度决定了曲率功率谱的截断尺度k_cut，使得SIGW峰值能够位于纳赫兹频带并具有可检测的振幅。对于三个代表性参数集，我们计算了SIGW谱，发现纳赫兹峰值的谱匹配NANOGrav和EPTA信号。通过选择不同参数，我们的模型同时预测了其他引力波探测器上可能观测到的信号。

## Abstract
We investigate scalar-induced gravitational waves (SIGWs) produced by domain walls (DWs) nucleated via quantum tunneling during inflation with an extended nucleation time. In contrast to the small-period nucleation framework, where DWs form instantaneously and produce a curvature power spectrum too weak to account for the nanohertz stochastic gravitational-wave background (SGWB) reported by pulsar timing array (PTA) collaborations, we show that a finite nucleation duration leads to a distribution of DW radii characterized by $γ\equiv\overline{R^4}/(\overline{R^2})^2>1$, which enhances the resulting curvature perturbations. We construct a two-field inflation model with an inflaton $φ$ and a spectator field $χ$ coupled through the potential $V(φ,χ)$, where the DW tension $σ(t)$ evolves smoothly as the inflaton rolls past a critical value. The characteristic width of this transition determines the cutoff scale $k_{\text{cut}}$ of the curvature power spectrum, enabling the SIGW peak to be placed in the nanohertz frequency band with detectable amplitude. For three representative parameter sets, we compute the SIGW spectra and find that the nanohertz-peaked spectrum matching the NANOGrav and EPTA signals. By selecting different parameters, our model simultaneously predicts potentially observable signals at other gravitational-wave detectors.
