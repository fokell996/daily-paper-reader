## 摘要
EP251023a是一个由EP独立探测到的河外快速X射线暂现源（eFXT），没有相应的伽马射线对应体。其瞬时辐射由持续时间为$T_{90}=292\pm19$秒的主辐射和随后持续到观测结束（$T_0+1571$秒）的长时尾辐射组成。利用Konus--Wind的上限，我们得到了主辐射阶段各向同性伽马射线能量$E_{\gamma,\rm{iso}}$的保守上限为$5.7 \times 10^{52}$ erg。从Keck光谱中的强吸收特征识别出红移$z = 2.232\pm0.001$，这些特征还表明宿主星系的中性氢柱密度相对较低。基于宽带能谱分布，晚期光变曲线显示一个无色散的平台，随后在大约49千秒处发生转折后出现斜率为3.99的极陡衰减，这与快速旋转的毫秒磁星引擎一致。在各向同性风模型下，我们得到磁星的初始周期$P_0<2.27$毫秒和磁场强度$B_p<8.33\times10^{14}$高斯；而考虑典型的0.1弧度张角的喷流准直时，这些约束放宽至$P_0<32.15$毫秒和$B_p<1.18\times10^{16}$高斯。与GRB 070707一起，EP251023a可能代表了一类罕见的光学磁星供能内平台事件，其外部激波污染很小，不同于以往主要在X射线中探测到的例子。未来对类似事件的发现将有助于澄清在光学波段观测到的磁星供能内部辐射与仅在X射线中探测到的辐射之间的关系。

## Abstract
EP251023a is an extragalactic fast X-ray transient (eFXT) detected solely by EP without a gamma-ray counterpart. The prompt emission consists of a main emission with a duration $T_{90}=292\pm19$ s, followed by a long-lasting tail emission that persists until the observation ends at $T_0+1571$ s. With the upper limit of Konus--Wind, we derived a conservative upper limit on the isotropic gamma-ray energy $E_{γ,\rm{iso}}$ of $5.7 \times 10^{52}$ erg for the main emission phase. A redshift of $z = 2.232\pm0.001$ is identified from strong absorption features in the Keck spectrum, which also indicate a relatively low host-galaxy HI column density. Based on the broadband spectral energy distribution, the late-time light curves show an achromatic plateau, followed by an extremely steep decay with a slope of 3.99 after a break at about 49 ks, which is consistent with a rapidly spinning millisecond magnetar engine. Under the isotropic wind scenario, we obtain the initial period $P_0<2.27$~ms and the magnetic field strength $B_p<8.33\times10^{14}$~G for the magnetar; whereas considering a jet collimation with a typical opening angle of 0.1 rad relaxes these constraints to $P_0<32.15$~ms and $B_p<1.18\times10^{16}$~G. Together with GRB\,070707, EP251023a may represent a rare class of optical magnetar-powered internal plateaus with little external-shock contamination, unlike previous examples detected primarily in X-rays. Future discoveries of similar events will help clarify the relationship between magnetar-powered internal emission observed in the optical band and that detected only in X-rays.

1. EP251023a 是一个红移 \(z=2.232\) 的河外快速 X 射线暂现源，无伽马射线对应体（\(E_{\gamma,\text{iso}}<5.7\times10^{52}\;\text{erg}\)，保守上限）。

6. 该发现表明未来爱因斯坦探针等巡天将有助于拓展此类事件样本，澄清磁星内部发射在光学与 X 射线波段的关系。
