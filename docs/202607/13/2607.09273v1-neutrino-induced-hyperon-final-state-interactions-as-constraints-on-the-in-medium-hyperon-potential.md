## 摘要
超子单粒子势 $U_Y(ρ)$ 控制着超子在原子核中的传播以及致密物质中超子的出现，它们会软化中子星的状态方程并降低最大质量——即“超子谜题”。我们表明，在 $^{40}$Ar 上的带电电流加速器（反）中微子相互作用，在原子核内产生 $Λ$ 和 $Σ$，可以约束这些势。在 SBND 和 DUNE 能量下，被俘获的 $Λ$ 比例和逃逸超子动量随 $U_Λ$ 和 $U_Σ$ 单调变化，而带有 Kaon 否决的 FSI-$Σ^+$ 标记增加了灵敏度。插入到已建立的超核/$Σ$-原子深度的 GM1 相对论平均场状态方程中，相同的势给出 $M_{\rm max} = 1.94\,M_\odot$ 和 $Λ_{1.4}=1034$，低于最重的脉冲星且高于 GW170817 约束的典型 GM1 类平均场。探测器级别的 Fisher 预测给出 $δU_Λ\simeq 0.3\,$MeV 和 $δU_Σ\simeq 3$-$4\,$MeV（对于固定的低密度指数 $γ$）。由于超子是在饱和密度以下产生的，$U_Λ(ρ_0)$ 和 $γ$ 有 99.8% 的反相关；对 $γ$ 边缘化后，锚点退化为 $δU_Λ\simeq 5.6\,$MeV（若先验 $±0.2$ 则为 $1.3\,$MeV），而 $δU_Σ$ 不变。对于 $U_Λ$，来自超子-核子末态截面（$-5/{+}2\,$MeV）和出射位移/梯度输运方案（$-6\,$MeV）的系统误差相当。对于 $U_Σ$，相同的 $YN$ 不确定性使拟合偏差达 $\mathcal{O}(150)\,$MeV；移除 $Σ^+$ 标记无法改善，因为 $Λ$ 动量谱承载了大部分 $U_Σ$ 信息，且本身对 $YN$ 敏感。低密度 $U_Λ$ 锚点是一个鲁棒的控制量，精度在几个 MeV 而非亚 MeV 量级。结合地面和重离子先验的联合拟合给出 $M_{\rm max} = 2.21^{+0.04}_{-0.15}\,\,$Msun，主要受外部 $c_Λ$ 先验设定。

## Abstract
Hyperon single-particle potentials $U_Y(ρ)$ control propagation in nuclei and hyperon onset in dense matter, where they soften the neutron-star equation of state and reduce the maximum mass -- the ``hyperon puzzle''. We show that charged-current accelerator (anti)neutrino interactions on $^{40}$Ar, producing $Λ$ and $Σ$ inside the nucleus, can constrain these potentials. At SBND and DUNE energies, the trapped-$Λ$ fraction and escaping-hyperon momenta vary monotonically with $U_Λ$ and $U_Σ$, with a kaon-vetoed FSI-$Σ^+$ tag adding sensitivity. Inserted in a GM1 relativistic mean-field equation of state at established hypernuclear/$Σ$-atom depths, the same potentials give $M_{\rm max} = 1.94\,M_\odot$ and $Λ_{1.4}=1034$, below the heaviest pulsars and above the GW170817 bound typical of GM1-class mean fields. A detector-level Fisher forecast yields $δU_Λ\simeq 0.3\,$MeV and $δU_Σ\simeq 3$-$4\,$MeV for fixed low-density exponent $γ$. Since hyperons are produced below saturation, $U_Λ(ρ_0)$ and $γ$ are 99.8\% anti-correlated; marginalising over $γ$ degrades the anchor to $δU_Λ\simeq 5.6\,$MeV ($1.3\,$MeV with a $\pm 0.2$ prior), while $δU_Σ$ is unchanged. For $U_Λ$, comparable systematics arise from hyperon-nucleon final-state cross sections ($-5/{+}2\,$MeV) and the exit-shift/gradient transport prescription ($-6\,$MeV). For $U_Σ$, the same $YN$ uncertainty biases the fit by $\mathcal{O}(150)\,$MeV; removing the $Σ^+$ tag does not cure this, because the $Λ$ momentum spectrum carries most $U_Σ$ information and is itself $YN$-sensitive. The low-density $U_Λ$ anchor is a robust handle, at several-MeV rather than sub-MeV precision. A joint fit with terrestrial and heavy-ion priors gives $M_{\rm max} = 2.21^{+0.04}_{-0.15}\,\,$Msun, set mainly by the external $c_Λ$ prior.
