## 摘要
快速解析和半解析的中子星研究通常需要一个便于计算的状态方程，同时产生的相对论性恒星序列需符合当前多信使约束。我们通过扫描一个光滑的双多方关系来构建这样的基准，该关系给出能量密度作为压力的函数：$\hatε(\hat p)=a_1\hat p^{Γ_1}+a_2\hat p^{Γ_2}$。利用基于大质量脉冲星、双中子星事件GW170817的潮汐形变能力以及NICER质量-半径测量值的滤波器来筛选参数。单个多方基准扫描未发现能通过所有滤波器的模型，而双多方扫描则识别出一个可行区域。然后，针对公开的NICER和GW170817后验数据集，使用曲线积分评分在此区域内选择基准状态方程。选出的代表支持$M_{\max}=2.44$--$2.49\,M_\odot$，$R_{1.4}\simeq 11.3$ km，$Λ_{1.4}=485$--$512$，并在稳定分支上保持因果性。这个紧凑的解析族为当前观测尺度下的相对论性恒星结构测试提供了参考案例。

## Abstract
Fast analytic and semi-analytic studies of neutron stars often require an equation of state that is convenient to evaluate while producing relativistic stellar sequences compatible with current multimessenger constraints. We construct such a benchmark by scanning a smooth double polytropic relation for the energy density as a function of pressure, $\hatε(\hat p)=a_1\hat p^{Γ_1}+a_2\hat p^{Γ_2}$. The parameters are selected with filters based on massive pulsars, tidal deformability from the binary-neutron-star event GW170817, and NICER mass-radius measurements. A single polytropic baseline scan finds no model passing all filters, whereas a double-polytrope scan identifies a viable region. A curve-integral score, evaluated against public NICER and GW170817 posterior data sets, is then used to choose benchmark equations of state within this region. The selected representatives support $M_{\max}=2.44$--$2.49\,M_\odot$, with $R_{1.4}\simeq 11.3$ km and $Λ_{1.4}=485$--$512$, and remain causal on the stable branch. This compact analytic family provides reference cases for relativistic stellar-structure tests at current observational scales.
