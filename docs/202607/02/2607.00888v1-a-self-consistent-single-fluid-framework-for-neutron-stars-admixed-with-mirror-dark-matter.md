## 摘要
我们基于一个接触矢量流-流相互作用构建了一个自洽框架，该相互作用通过相互的平均场位移耦合两个扇区的化学势，其中暗物质份额$F_D = N_D/N_B$作为全局输入参数固定。这一公式为固定密度处方提供了物理动机明确的替代方案，允许局部暗物质密度在整个恒星内部跟随重子物质密度。作为应用，我们考虑暗物质与可见扇区具有精确对称性的镜像暗物质场景，并使用NL3$ωρ$、FSU2R、NL3和DDME2物态方程研究中子星物质。我们发现暗物质-重子物质相互作用削弱了致密物质的结合，降低了其不可压缩性，并软化了物态方程。因此，暗物质增加了中子星的中心密度和致密性，降低了其最大质量，并将直接Urca过程的起始点移至更高的恒星密度。因此，对于具有硬对称能的模型，快速冷却的起始点被移至更大质量恒星；而对于具有软对称能的模型，则移至更小质量恒星，具体取决于暗物质掺入导致的额外致密性。这些结果表明，镜像暗物质掺入既改变了中子星的微观组成，也改变了其宏观结构，对其热演化和多信使观测特征具有潜在影响。

## Abstract
We develop a self-consistent framework based on a contact vector current-current interaction that couples the chemical potentials of both sectors through mutual mean-field shifts, with the dark matter (DM) fraction $F_D = N_D/N_B$ fixed as a global input parameter. This formulation provides a physically motivated alternative to fixed-density prescriptions, allowing the local DM density to follow the baryonic matter (BM) density throughout the stellar interior. As an application, we consider a mirror-DM scenario with exact symmetry between the dark and visible sectors and investigate NS matter using the NL3$ωρ$, FSU2R, NL3, and DDME2 equations of state (EOSs). We find that the DM--BM interaction weakens the binding of dense matter, reduces its incompressibility, and softens the EOS. Consequently, DM increases the central density and compactness of NSs, lowers their maximum masses, and shifts the onset of the direct Urca process to higher stellar densities. As a consequence, the onset of rapid cooling is shifted to more massive stars for models with a stiff symmetry energy and to less massive stars for models with a soft symmetry energy, depending on the extra compactness that results from the DM admixture. These results demonstrate that mirror-DM admixtures modify both the microscopic composition and macroscopic structure of NSs, with potential implications for their thermal evolution and multimessenger observational signatures.
