## 摘要
我们利用MeerKAT脉冲星计时阵列（MPTA）的4.5年数据发布给出了引力子质量的约束，并为即将到来的平方公里阵列PTA（SKA–PTA）提供了预测。通过建模有质量引力子的修正色散关系和相应的张量相关函数，我们在三种噪声配置（DATA、ER、ALT）下对角度相关测量进行了贝叶斯推断。引力子质量的90%置信上限分别为：$m_g < 2.10 \times 10^{-23}\,\mathrm{eV}/c^2$ (DATA)、$m_g < 2.58 \times 10^{-23}\,\mathrm{eV}/c^2$ (ER) 和 $m_g < 2.25 \times 10^{-23}\,\mathrm{eV}/c^2$ (ALT)。包含单极子和偶极子贡献并不会显著改变这些界限，确认了约束由四极子张量相关主导。所有结果与广义相对论完全一致。对于SKA–PTA，我们预测在10年观测基线下的灵敏度可达$m_g \sim 10^{-24}\,\mathrm{eV}/c^2$，在50年观测基线下达$m_g \sim 10^{-25}\,\mathrm{eV}/c^2$，相对于当前极限有数量级提升。这项工作展示了当前和未来的PTA观测在纳赫兹波段检验引力基本方面的能力。

## Abstract
We present constraints on the graviton mass using the 4.5-year data release from the MeerKAT Pulsar Timing Array (MPTA) and provide forecasts for the upcoming Square Kilometre Array PTA (SKA--PTA). By modeling the modified dispersion relation and the corresponding tensor correlation function for massive gravitons, we perform Bayesian inference on the angular-correlation measurements under three noise configurations (DATA, ER, ALT). Our 90\% credible upper limits on the graviton mass are $m_g < 2.10 \times 10^{-23}\,\mathrm{eV}/c^{2}$ (DATA), $m_g < 2.58 \times 10^{-23}\,\mathrm{eV}/c^{2}$ (ER), and $m_g < 2.25 \times 10^{-23}\,\mathrm{eV}/c^{2}$ (ALT). Including monopolar and dipolar contributions does not significantly alter these bounds, confirming that the constraints are driven by the quadrupolar tensor correlation. All results remain fully consistent with general relativity. For SKA--PTA, we forecast sensitivities down to $m_g \sim 10^{-24}\,\mathrm{eV}/c^{2}$ with a 10-year observing baseline and $m_g \sim 10^{-25}\,\mathrm{eV}/c^{2}$ with a 50-year observing baseline, representing order-of-magnitude improvements over current limits. This work demonstrates the power of current and future PTA observations to test fundamental aspects of gravity in the nanohertz band.
