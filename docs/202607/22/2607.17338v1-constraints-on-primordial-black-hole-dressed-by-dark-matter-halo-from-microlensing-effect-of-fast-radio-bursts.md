## 摘要
原初黑洞（PBH）不仅被视为暗物质候选体，还被LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA视为双黑洞并合引力波的潜在来源，以及詹姆斯·韦布空间望远镜观测到的超大质量黑洞的种子，因此在宇宙学和天体物理学中持续引起强烈兴趣。快速射电暴（FRB）是明亮的毫秒级射电瞬变现象，其物理起源仍不明确，但已迅速发展为天文学中最活跃、发展最快的领域之一。FRB的微引力透镜效应为探测PBH提供了干净而有力的手段，尤其对于恒星质量窗口以上的质量范围。在本工作中，我们推导出一个完整变换，将原本针对单色质量分布“裸”PBH推导出的丰度上限，转化为对具有任意扩展质量分布的“包裹”PBH的相应约束。基于这一框架，我们估计了未来利用FRB观测对包裹PBH丰度f_PBH的约束，假设在下一个十年内观测到10^5个FRB样本，这完全在SKA的预期探测能力范围内。我们的结果表明，包含暗物质晕增强会使f_PBH的上限收紧约一个数量级，对于从恒星质量到中等质量黑洞的典型质量范围，最严格的约束可达~10^{-4}。

## Abstract
Primordial black holes (PBHs) are not only considered as a candidate for dark matter, but also as potential sources of gravitational waves from binary black hole mergers by the LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA and as seeds for the supermassive black holes observed by the James-Webb Space Telescope, thereby remaining intense interest in cosmology and astrophysics. Fast radio bursts (FRBs) are bright millisecond-duration radio transients whose physical origin remains elusive, which have rapidly developed into one of the most active and rapidly evolving fields in astronomy. The microlensing effect of FRBs offers a clean and powerful probe of PBHs, especially in the mass range above stellar-mass window. In this work, we derive a complete transformation that converts any upper limit on the abundance of PBHs originally derived for `bare' PBHs with monochromatic mass distribution, into the corresponding constraint on `dressed' PBHs with arbitrary extended mass distributions. Based on this framework, we estimate the future constraints on the dressed PBH abundance \(f_{\mathrm{PBH}}\) from FRB observations assuming an expected sample of \(10^5\) FRBs accumulated over the next decade well within the projected detection capabilities of SKA. Our results indicate that including halo enhancement tightens the upper limits on \(f_{\mathrm{PBH}}\) by approximately one order of magnitude, with the most stringent constraint reaching \(\sim10^{-4}\) for the typical mass range from stellar-mass to intermediate-mass black holes.
