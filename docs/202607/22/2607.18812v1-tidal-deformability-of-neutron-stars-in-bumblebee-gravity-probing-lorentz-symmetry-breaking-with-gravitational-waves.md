## 摘要
我们研究了蜂鸟引力中Neves-Gardim分支下静态中子星的潮汐性质。蜂鸟引力是一种矢量-张量理论，其中蜂鸟场的非零真空期望值自发破缺了局域洛伦兹对称性。星体背景由完整的修正Tolman-Oppenheimer-Volkoff系统确定，包括包含$m''(r)$的项。对于可微分的正压状态方程，该系统在不丢弃任何蜂鸟场相关贡献的情况下被重新表示为代数等价的一阶形式。由于目前尚无法对该星体分支进行静态偶宇称度量和蜂鸟场扰动的完整耦合推导，潮汐部分通过显式的有效度规变形处方处理：Hinderer流体扰动方程在修正的蜂鸟背景下计算，并保留标准表面匹配关系作为同一处方的一部分。我们计算了BSk20、BSk21、DD2和MS1状态方程在$\ell\in[-0.4,+1.0]$范围内的四极潮汐Love数$k_2$和无量纲潮汐形变参数$\Lambda=(2/3)k_2\mathcal{C}^{-5}$。在基准计算中，广义相对论极限在$\ell=0$处以优于$0.2\%$的精度恢复。在所采用的有效处方内，与GW170817双星潮汐形变界限$\widetilde\Lambda\leq720$的比较给出了依赖于状态方程的极限值$\ell_{\max}=+0.25$（BSk20）、$+0.06$（BSk21）、$-0.09$（DD2）和$-0.25$（MS1）。这些潮汐界限以所述扰动处方的条件为前提，当完整的线性化矢量-张量问题可用时应重新评估。

## Abstract
We investigate the tidal properties of static neutron stars in the Neves--Gardim branch of bumblebee gravity, a vector--tensor theory in which a nonzero vacuum expectation value of the bumblebee field spontaneously breaks local Lorentz symmetry. The stellar background is determined from the full modified Tolman--Oppenheimer--Volkoff system, including the term containing $m''(r)$. For a differentiable barotropic equation of state, this system is recast into an algebraically equivalent first-order form without discarding any bumblebee-dependent contribution. Because a complete coupled derivation of static even-parity metric and bumblebee-field perturbations is not presently available for this stellar branch, the tidal sector is treated through an explicit effective metric-deformation prescription: the Hinderer fluid perturbation equation is evaluated on the modified bumblebee background, and the standard surface matching relation is retained as part of the same prescription. We compute the quadrupolar tidal Love number $k_2$ and the dimensionless tidal deformability $Λ=(2/3)k_2\mathcal{C}^{-5}$ for the BSk20, BSk21, DD2, and MS1 equations of state over $\ell\in[-0.4,+1.0]$. The general-relativistic limit is recovered at $\ell=0$ to within $0.2\%$ in the benchmark calculations. Within the adopted effective prescription, comparison with the GW170817 binary tidal-deformability bound $\widetildeΛ\leq720$ yields the EOS-dependent limiting values $\ell_{\max}=+0.25$ (BSk20), $+0.06$ (BSk21), $-0.09$ (DD2), and $-0.25$ (MS1). These tidal limits are conditional on the stated perturbative prescription and should be reassessed when the complete linearised vector--tensor problem becomes available.
