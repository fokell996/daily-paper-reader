## 摘要
在本研究中，我们在f(R,T)修正引力的框架下，研究了耦合参数、磁场和各向异性参数对中子星结构的影响。本文采用基于AV18核子-核子势的实际微观计算导出的精确状态方程，来计算这一致密天体的结构。在f(R,T)引力中，施瓦西半径、致密度、引力表面红移和Kretschmann标量的确定，证实了我们的理论结果与观测约束一致。虽然爱因斯坦引力框架内已建立的状态方程已成功描述了广泛的致密天体，但在解释位于质量间隙（2.5至5太阳质量）内的某些大质量天体时仍显不足。我们表明，在f(R,T)引力框架下，一些位于质量间隙内的致密天体可被解释为中子星候选体。最后，我们将结果与LIGO/Virgo/KAGRA和NICER的观测数据进行比较，通过设定f(R,T)理论和各向异性的参数，成功再现了GW170817、PSR J0952-0607和PSR J0740+6620的质量和半径，以及GW190814和GW200210-092254的次级成分质量。

## Abstract
In this study, within the framework of f(R,T) modified gravity, we investigate the influence of coupling parameter, magnetic field and anisotropy parameter on the neutron star structure. This work employs an accurate equation of state (EoS), derived from realistic microscopic calculations based on the AV18 nucleon-nucleon potential, to compute the structure of this compact object. Here, determination of Schwarzschild radius, compactness, gravitational surface redshift and Kretschmann scalar within the f(R, T) gravity, confirms that our theoretical results are consistent with the observational constraints. While established physical EoSs within the framework of Einstein gravity have successfully characterized a broad range of compact objects, they remain inadequate in explaining certain massive objects residing within the mass gap (2.5 to 5 Msun). We show that some compact objects residing in the mass gap interpreted as candidates of neutron stars within the framework of f(R, T) gravity. Finally, we compare our results with the observational data from LIGO/Virgo/KAGRA and NICER, setting the parameters of the f(R, T) theory and anisotropy to successfully reproduce the masses and radii of the GW170817, PSR J0952-0607 and PSR J0740+6620 and the masses of the secondary components of GW190814 and GW200210-092254.
