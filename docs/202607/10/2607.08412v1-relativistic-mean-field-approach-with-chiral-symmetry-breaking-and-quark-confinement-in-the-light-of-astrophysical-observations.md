## 摘要
我们对相对论平均场方法进行了贝叶斯分析，该方法实现了手征禁闭模型，同时包含了手征对称性破缺和禁闭效应，并且最近被证明能够很好地再现有限原子核的基态性质。此外，我们探索了ρ和ω介子之间耦合以及非线性ω耦合的影响。我们的模型同时受到近饱和密度核物质性质、多信使中子星天体物理观测以及/或格点QCD对核子质量预测的约束。在同时再现约2倍太阳质量的大质量中子星和GW170817推断的潮汐形变能力时，模型表现出张力。我们显示，贝叶斯因子分析所支持的额外ωρ耦合显著缓解了这种张力，而对于RMF-CC模型来说，添加非线性ω自相互作用并非必要。由于手征动力学对标量部分施加的强约束以及我们处理禁闭所导致的高密度状态方程变软，RMF-CC方法倾向于较硬的状态方程。由于我们没有考虑中子星核心中的相变，这种硬化是通过约300 MeV的大不可压缩模量值实现的。最后，我们将著名的RMF模型与具有相同约束的RMF-CC模型进行比较，发现在中子星核心没有相变的情况下，模型更偏好RMF模型。

## Abstract
We perform a Bayesian analysis of a relativistic mean-field approach, which is an implementation of the chiral confining model with both chiral symmetry breaking and confinement effects, and which was recently proven to reproduce well the ground state properties of finite nuclei. We additionally explore the impact of couplings between $ρ$ and $ω$ mesons as well as a non-linear $ω$ coupling. Our models are simultaneously constrained by nuclear matter properties near saturation density, multi-messenger neutron star astrophysical observations, and/or lattice QCD predictions of the nucleon mass. It exhibits tension in simultaneously reproducing the $\sim 2M_{\odot}$ massive NS and the tidal deformability inferred from GW170817. We show that an additional $ωρ$ coupling, favored by Bayes factor analysis, substantially alleviates this tension, while adding a non-linear $ω$ self-interaction is not necessary for the RMF-CC model. Owing to the strong constraints on the scalar sector imposed by chiral dynamics and the softening of the equation of state at high densities induced by our treatment of confinement, the RMF-CC approach favors stiff equations of state. Since we do not consider phase transition in the core of neutron stars, this stiffening is obtained with large values of the incompressibility modulus of about $\sim300$ MeV. We finally compare the well-known RMF model with RMF-CC models with the same constraints, and we obtain a preference for the RMF model in the absence of a phase transition in the core of neutron stars.
