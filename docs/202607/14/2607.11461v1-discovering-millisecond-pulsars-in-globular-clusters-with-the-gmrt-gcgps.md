## 摘要
球状星团GMRT脉冲星巡天（GCGPS）项目于2023年5月中旬启动，已成为使用升级版巨型米波射电望远镜（uGMRT）进行的最成功的脉冲星巡天之一，发现了数颗新的毫秒脉冲星（MSPs）。最近结束的第一阶段巡天在四个球状星团（GCs）中发现了七颗MSPs，这些星团此前均未有已知脉冲星。这些发现首次使得精确测定各星团的色散量（DMs）成为可能。持续的时间跟随观测显示，在这七颗MSPs中，三颗被确认为双星，两颗为孤立脉冲星。本文总结了GCGPS项目第一阶段的设计与实施，介绍了迄今取得的关键科学成果，并概述了近期启动的第二阶段观测的策略与进展。

## Abstract
The Globular Clusters GMRT Pulsar Search (GCGPS) project, launched in mid-May 2023, has emerged as one of the most successful pulsar surveys conducted with the upgraded Giant Metrewave Radio Telescope (uGMRT), leading to the discovery of several new millisecond pulsars (MSPs). The recently concluded Phase I of the survey resulted in the discovery of seven MSPs across four globular clusters (GCs), all of which previously had no known pulsars. These discoveries have enabled the precise determination of the dispersion measures (DMs) for the respective clusters for the first time. Consistent timing follow-up revealed that out of the seven MSPs, three are confirmed binaries, while two are isolated. This paper summarises the design and the implementation of Phase I of the GCGPS project, presents the key scientific results obtained so far, and outlines the strategy and progress of the recently initiated Phase II observations.
