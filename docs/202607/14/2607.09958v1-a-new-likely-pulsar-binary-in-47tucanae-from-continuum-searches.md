## 摘要
我们提出证据表明，长期被认为是日冕活动驱动的X射线源47 Tuc W41实际上是一个红背脉冲星双星。该源持续显示LX = 3 × 10^31 erg s^{-1}（0.5-10 keV），远超活跃双星在宁静期的日冕饱和极限，但与其光子指数Γ=1.4±0.1一致，符合红背脉冲星中观测到的双星内激波特征。此外，利用澳大利亚望远镜致密阵列的深度数据，我们发现W41是一个微弱（5.5 GHz处9.8±1.4 μJy）、陡谱（α= -1.8±0.4）的射电连续谱源，符合脉冲星的预期。哈勃空间望远镜测光数据的光变曲线建模显示了椭球调制的证据，并伴有对约0.5-0.55 M⊙的伴星围绕不可见天体的轻度照射，这也与红背脉冲星解释一致。W41的精确位置与其精确测量的10.4小时轨道周期相结合，将有助于在未来数据中将其与新发现的射电脉冲星匹配。我们的结果表明，即使在像47 Tuc这样研究充分的星团中，紧密的脉冲星双星仍然隐藏着，并被错误分类为活跃双星。

## Abstract
We present evidence that the X-ray source 47~Tuc~W41, long considered to be an X-ray source powered by coronal activity, is actually a redback pulsar binary. The source continually shows $L_X = 3 \times 10^{31}$ erg s$^{-1}$ (0.5--10 keV), which is well in excess of the coronal saturation limit for active binaries in quiescence, but is consistent with the intrabinary shock observed in redback pulsars, as is its photon index of $Γ= 1.4\pm0.1$. In addition, using deep data from the Australia Telescope Compact Array, we show that W41 is a faint ($9.8\pm1.4 μ$Jy at 5.5 GHz) steep spectrum ($α= -1.8\pm0.4$) radio continuum source, as expected for a pulsar. Light curve modelling of Hubble Space Telescope photometry shows evidence for ellipsoidal modulation with mild irradiation of a $\sim 0.5$--$0.55 M_{\odot}$ secondary around an invisible companion, also consistent with the redback interpretation. The precise position of W41, along with its well-measured 10.4-hr orbital period, should enable it to be matched to a newly-discovered radio pulsar in future data. Our result shows that close pulsar binaries are still hiding, misclassified among active binaries, even in well-studied clusters like 47 Tuc.
